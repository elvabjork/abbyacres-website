SUMMER CAMP 2019 PRICES

We offer other dates when available. You just need to make a request.

There are up to 24 camper slots available each week. Applicants may sign-up as individuals or groups. Be advised that individuals and/or small groups will be scheduled along with other campers during their week to fill remaining slots.
For 2019, registration will begin in February, with a non-refundable $50 deposit due by March 31st. Registration for remaining slots after March 31st requires an immediate deposit. Total cost is $200 per camper. Remaining balances are due prior to start of camp.
