CAMP STAFF

SUMMER PROGRAM CAMP STAFF

Anyone 16 years or older (must have completed 10th grade by camp start date) can apply for summer camp counselor, worship leader, and/or equestrian activity leader, etc. These are unpaid volunteer positions, but room and board, as well as camp T-shirts, etc., are all provided by the camp.
While we give preference to counselors who can serve for the full 5-6 week summer season, we will be accepting part time counselors who are available during one of our 3 to 4 week long summer periods.

Applications can be requested by phone, mail, or can be completed online. Personal references, background check, and personal statement of faith are required for all applicants. Initial application due no later than April 30th, however early applicants will receive first consideration.
